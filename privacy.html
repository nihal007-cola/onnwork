> PRIVACY POLICY

// last updated: december 2025

Offices ONN ("we", "our", "us") provides workflow and messaging automation services using tools such as Google Sheets, Google Apps Script, and the WhatsApp Business Cloud API.

What data we collect
Names of customers, suppliers, and workers (e.g., karigars), Mobile phone numbers, Business-related information such as pending jobslips, WIP status, payment status, Technical logs (message IDs, timestamps, delivery results) generated by WhatsApp or our automation scripts.

How we use this data
To send WhatsApp or email notifications configured by you, To maintain logs of message delivery, failures, and system activity, To improve system reliability and identify operational issues.

Data sharing
We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with service providers required to operate the system (e.g., Google, Meta/WhatsApp) and authorized members of your organization.

Data retention
Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service and for basic troubleshooting. You may delete data from your systems at any time.

Your rights
If you are an individual whose data is being processed and wish to have your data removed, you may contact us. We will coordinate with the respective business to remove your data.
